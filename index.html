Our Smart Services

Smart Locks

Keyless entry, remote access, and secure fingerprint authentication.

Smart locks provide advanced security with remote access, real-time monitoring, and biometric authentication. Biometric Locks: These locks use fingerprint or facial recognition technology for access.

A smart lock is an electronic lock that allows you to lock and unlock doors using various digital methods like a smartphone app, voice command, fingerprint, or key fob, instead of a physical key. They can be integrated into a smart home system and offer remote control, monitoring, and access management.

Smart Switch

Control lighting and appliances via app or voice command.

Smart switches allow seamless control of lights and devices using your phone or voice assistant. Smart switches are programmable wireless devices that replace traditional light switches, offering remote control, voice activation, and scheduling of lights and other connected appliances. They connect to your home's Wi-Fi or Bluetooth network, allowing you to manage them via a smartphone app or voice commands.

Smart Lighting

Set moods, schedules, and brightness with your phone.

Customize ambience, automate schedules, and remotely control lighting to suit your lifestyle.

Smart lighting refers to lighting systems that can be controlled remotely, often through a smartphone app, voice commands, or smart home systems.

Curtains

Automate curtain open/close with time or sunlight control.

Motorized curtains that adjust automatically based on schedules or sensors, bringing style and efficiency to your space.

Smart curtains refer to motorised curtains that can be conveniently operated through various means such as a remote control, voice commands, or a mobile app. They offer automation capabilities that enhance the convenience and comfort of your home.

AC Control

Remote temperature and comfort control at your fingertips.

Manage your home's cooling for optimal comfort and energy savings with smart AC control.

The ability to manage your air conditioner's settings and operation using a smartphone app or voice commands, often via a Wi-Fi-enabled controller or a smart AC unit. This allows for remote access, scheduling, and integration with smart home ecosystems.

Music

Stream music wirelessly throughout your home with multi-room audio systems.

Smart music control refers to the ability to remotely manage and interact with audio playback, often through smartphone apps or smart home systems. These systems offer features like mood-based playlists, remote control, and integration with various audio devices.

Security Cameras

Monitor your home 24/7 with smart surveillance.

Advanced camera systems with motion alerts, cloud storage, and remote access for peace of mind wherever you are.

Smart security cameras are surveillance cameras that can be connected to the internet and controlled remotely via smartphone or other devices. They offer features like motion detection, facial recognition, and night vision, often with the ability to send alerts and notifications.

Smart Sensors

Automate responses to motion, temperature, or humidity.

Sensors detect environmental changes and trigger automated responses like lighting or climate adjustments.

Smart home sensors are devices that monitor various aspects of a home's environment and activity, providing alerts and information to users via a smartphone app or other smart device. These sensors can detect things like fire, water leaks, intrusions, and changes in air quality, acting as a layer of protection and peace of mind for homeowners.

Voice Assistant

Control your smart home using simple voice commands.

Use Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri to manage your devices with voice commands.

To control a home automation solution through voice, you'll need a voice assistant like Google Assistant or Amazon Alexa, smart devices compatible with your chosen assistant, and a smart hub to connect them all. This setup allows you to use voice commands to control lights, thermostats, and other smart devices.

Why Smart Homes Are the Future

Automation is revolutionizing our homes by improving energy efficiency, comfort, and security. With devices like smart thermostats and lighting systems, homeowners enjoy enhanced control and convenience.

Smart technology is evolving fast, making homes not just intelligent, but intuitive—predicting needs and optimizing energy use automatically.

Enhanced Convenience and Automation:

1.Remote Control: Homeowners can control various aspects of their homes, like lighting, temperature, and appliances, from anywhere with an internet connection.

2.Automation: Smart homes can automate tasks like adjusting the thermostat based on occupancy or turning on lights at specific times, saving time and effort.

3.Personalization: Smart home systems can be customized to individual preferences, creating a more comfortable and tailored living environment.

4. Increased Security and Safety:

a. Real-time Monitoring: Smart security systems, including cameras and motion detectors, provide real-time monitoring and alerts, enhancing home security.

b. Remote Access: Homeowners can monitor their homes remotely, providing peace of mind and the ability to take action if needed.

5.Advanced Features:

Smart homes can incorporate advanced security features like smart locks and alarms, further enhancing safety.

6.Real-time Monitoring and Insights:

Smart homes provide insights into energy consumption patterns, allowing homeowners to identify areas where they can save energy.

Top 5 Smart Devices You Need

From smart plugs to voice-controlled assistants, the top five smart devices can significantly boost your home's functionality without breaking the bank.

These include smart lights, thermostats, security cameras, voice assistants, and door locks—all easily integrated and user-friendly.

Enhanced Convenience and Automation:

1. Smart Speaker/Voice Assistant: A smart speaker with a voice assistant like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri is a central command center for your smart home. It allows you to control other smart devices with voice commands, such as turning lights on and off, adjusting the thermostat, or locking doors

2. Smart Lighting System: Smart lighting, like Philips Hue, allows you to control your lights remotely and create different lighting scenarios, like a relaxing ambiance or a bright, energy-efficient setting. You can also control your lighting with voice commands or set schedule

3. Smart Thermostat: A smart thermostat, like the Nest or EcoBee, learns your temperature preferences and automatically adjusts the temperature, helping you save energy and stay comfortable. You can also control the thermostat remotely via a smartphone app or voice commands

4. Smart Security Camera: A smart security camera, like Ring or Wyze Cam, provides real-time monitoring of your home and sends alerts when motion is detected. You can also view live footage and recordings remotely

5. Smart Lock: A smart lock, like August or Kwikset, allows you to remotely lock and unlock your doors, grant temporary access to guests, and track access history. You can also control the lock with voice commands or a smartphone app
